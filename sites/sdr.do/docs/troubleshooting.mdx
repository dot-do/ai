# Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions for SDR agents.

## Low Response Rates

**Problem**: Leads not responding to outreach

**Solutions**:

- Improve personalization in messages
- Test different subject lines and opening lines
- Adjust timing of outreach
- Try different channels (email, LinkedIn, phone)
- Refine targeting to reach better-fit leads

## Poor Qualification Rates

**Problem**: Leads not qualifying as sales-ready

**Solutions**:

- Review and tighten qualification criteria
- Target higher-intent leads
- Improve lead scoring model
- Focus on specific industries or company sizes

## Low Show Rates

**Problem**: Prospects not showing up for booked meetings

**Solutions**:

- Send automated reminders
- Make value proposition clearer
- Reduce friction in booking process
- Follow up to confirm attendance

## Email Deliverability

**Problem**: Emails going to spam or not being delivered

**Solutions**:

- Verify SMTP configuration
- Check sender reputation
- Avoid spam trigger words
- Warm up email domain
- Use email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)

## CRM Sync Issues

**Problem**: Data not syncing correctly with CRM

**Solutions**:

- Verify API credentials
- Check rate limits
- Review field mappings
- Monitor sync logs
