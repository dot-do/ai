# Architecture

SDR agent architecture for autonomous lead qualification and prospecting.

## Core Components

### 1. Lead Researcher

Gathers intelligence on leads and accounts through web research, social media, and company data.

### 2. Prospector

Identifies and reaches out to potential customers using multi-channel approaches.

### 3. Qualifier

Evaluates lead fit using BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timing) or custom criteria.

### 4. Meeting Scheduler

Books meetings with qualified prospects and manages calendar coordination.

## AI Reasoning Engine

SDR agents use GPT-5 or Claude Sonnet 4.5 for:

- Lead research and analysis
- Personalized outreach messaging
- Qualification assessment
- Objection handling
- Meeting scheduling

## Integration Architecture

### CRM Integration

- Salesforce, HubSpot for lead tracking
- Automatic lead scoring and routing
- Activity logging

### Communication Tools

- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Email SMTP for outreach
- Calendar integration for scheduling
